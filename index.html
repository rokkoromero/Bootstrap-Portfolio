About Me

Are you looking for a home in San Diego? Famous for its pleasant climate, diverse population,and more than 70 miles of coastline, San Diego is heaven on Earth. You can swim, surf, hike, and sightsee to your heart’s content. Not the outdoorsy type? San Diego also offers a vibrant metropolitan life suited for city dwellers. The area caters to both families and single professionals seeking to put down roots. Owning San Diego property is a tremendous privilege – allow me to show you why.

I’m Rokko Romero, a realtor and one of the many residents of this great county. My passion in real estate stems from my experiences in owning and renting out properties. Specializing in real estate negotiation, I’m eager to utilize my professional training and knowledge of the area to assist you in making your decision. My goal is to help you find your ideal home and maximize the value derived from your investment. My promise to you is singular: I will use my expertise and resources to help you make the best purchase. Your buying experience should be fun, exciting, and hassle-free. That’s what I’m here for.

Location, location, location. Featuring a unique mix of natural environments and bustling suburban jungles, San Diego is unrivaled in the riches it offers. Real estate value continue to skyrocket in San Diego due to high demand and capital infusion. Biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, aerospace, and manufacturing industries ensure the long-term economic health of the area. San Diego will experience exponential land appreciation in the coming years, thus making your potential investment highly profitable.

What should I know before investing in real estate? When is the best time to purchase property? What locations will yield the highest value? These are just some of the questions I’m here to answer. Let’s get started!
